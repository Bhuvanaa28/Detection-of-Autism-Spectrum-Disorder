CHILD 4-11(AUTISM SPECTRUM DISORDER TEST)

1. She/he often notices small sounds when others do not:

             

2. She/he usually concentrates more on the whole picture, rather than the small details

           

3. In a social group, s/he can easily keep track of several different people’s conversations

           

4. She/he finds it easy to go back and forth between different activities

           

5. She/he doesn’t know how to keep a conversation going with his/her peers

           

6. She/he is good at social chit-chat

           

7. When she/he is read a story, s/he finds it difficult to work out the character’s intentions or feelings

           

8. When she/he was in preschool, she/he used to enjoy playing games involving pretending with other children

           

9. She/he finds it easy to work out what someone is thinking or feeling just by looking at their face

           

10. She/he finds it hard to make new friends
